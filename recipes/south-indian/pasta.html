Pasta

Boil pasta until soft, then drain the water.

Add your favorite sauce, mix well, and enjoy!

Description:

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Olive oil (optional)
  • Veggies
  • Your favorite sauce

Instructions:

  1. Boil water in a large pot.
  2. Add a pinch of salt to the boiling water.
  3. Add pasta to the boiling water.
  4. Cook according to package instructions (usually 8-12 minutes).
  5. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  6. Once pasta is cooked, drain the water using a colander.
  7. Optionally, drizzle with olive oil to prevent sticking.
  8. Add your favorite sauce and mix well.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!
