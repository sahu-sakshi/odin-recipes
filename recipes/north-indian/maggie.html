Maggie

Description:

Boil Maggie until Soft

Add the tastemaker and mix well

Enjoy your Maggie!

Ingredients:

  • 1 Packet Maggie
  • 2 Cups Water
  • Veggies (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a pan.
  2. Add Maggie noodles to the boiling water.
  3. Cook for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the tastemaker (spice mix) that comes with the Maggie packet.
  5. Mix well and cook for another 2 minutes until the noodles are soft and cooked through.
  6. If desired, add chopped veggies like carrots, peas, or bell peppers for added nutrition and flavor.
  7. Once cooked, turn off the heat and let it sit for a minute.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Maggie!
